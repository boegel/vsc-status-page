Planned maintenance

Storage firmware updates, disaster recovery procedure evaluation and maintenance

Start: 2024-07-02 07:00:00
End: 2024-07-05 18:00:00
Updated: 2024-06-26 03:45:25
Level: high

We have scheduled a downtime for all CalcUA clusters between Tuesday July 2, 7 am, and Friday July 5, 6 pm.

This is needed to finalize changes to our storage system (firmware updates), as a follow-up measure for the emergency shutdown from May 1.
We will also evaluate our emergency (disaster recovery) procedures and add extra detection systems.
Furthermore, we will also perform some other maintenance tasks.
And finally, we will run some tests before we release the clusters again.

During the first phase (firmware updates + emergency procedures), our filesystems will not be available.
This implies that access to other VSC Tier-2 clusters will not be possible.

Access to VSC Tier-1 is possible, but you will not have access to your $VSC_HOME and $VSC_DATA directories.
We estimate that this first phase will not take more than 2 days.

During the second phase, the filesystems will be available again, and as such access to other VSC Tier-2 clusters should be possible again.
But during the maintenance tasks, it will not be possible to submit and run jobs on our CalcUA clusters.

We will let you know when the filesystems are online again and when you can again submit jobs to the CalcUA clusters.

Our apologies for the inconvenience.
